Privacy Policy

Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy outlines how ByteCart collects, uses, and protects your personal information when using our website and services.

1. What Information We Collect

We collect the following types of personal data when you interact with our platform:

  • Name and email address
  • Billing and shipping address
  • Order history and preferences
  • Contact details when you submit forms or reach out to support

2. How We Use Your Data

Your data is used to:

  • Process orders and transactions
  • Communicate with you regarding purchases or account activity
  • Improve our services and personalize user experience
  • Ensure website functionality and security

3. Use of Cookies

We use cookies to remember your preferences, analyze site traffic, and enhance functionality. You may disable cookies through your browser settings, but parts of the site may not function properly.

4. Sharing Your Information

We never sell your data. We may share information with trusted third-party services (like payment processors or delivery companies) solely to complete your transaction or support your experience.

5. Data Security

We take data protection seriously and use technical and organizational measures to safeguard your information. However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to request:

  • Access to your personal data
  • Correction of inaccurate data
  • Deletion of your account and associated data

To exercise these rights, please contact us.

7. Policy Updates

This Privacy Policy may be updated to reflect changes in our practices. We recommend checking this page periodically. The last update was on 16 July 2025.

For any concerns or questions, please get in touch.
